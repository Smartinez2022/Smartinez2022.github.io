Our Mission

To empower students with the knowledge and tools to excel academically through the responsible use of Artificial Intelligence, fostering innovation, creativity, and critical thinking.

About The Project

Our Goal

At LAUSD, our goal is to empower students with the tools and knowledge to thrive in a rapidly evolving digital world. This initiative aligns with LAUSD's commitment to digital equity by ensuring all students have access to cutting-edge technologies like Artificial Intelligence. By fostering responsible AI use, we aim to bridge the digital divide, promote innovation, and prepare students for academic and professional success in the 21st century.

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Definition

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, and problem-solving.

How to Use AI Effectively

Tips and Guidelines

Clearly define what you want to achieve with AI, whether it's research, content creation, or problem-solving.

Provide detailed and precise prompts to get accurate and relevant responses from AI tools.

Cross-check AI-generated content with reliable sources to ensure accuracy and credibility.

Treat AI as a support system to enhance your work, not as a substitute for critical thinking or creativity.

If the AI's response isn't satisfactory, refine your input or ask follow-up questions for better results.

Avoid sharing sensitive or personal information with AI tools.
